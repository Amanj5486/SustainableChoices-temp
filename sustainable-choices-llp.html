About Us

Sustainable Choices LLP is a newly established firm based in Pune, Maharashtra, dedicated to bringing exceptional Indian handicrafts and artisanal products to the design and furnishing sector. We work at the intersection of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary interior design.

As emerging volume buyers in the handicrafts industry, we are committed to sourcing authentic, high-quality products that blend sustainability with aesthetic excellence. Our focus lies in creating meaningful connections between skilled artisans and design-conscious clients.
